What is Unsane

Unsane is a dark solo, multiplayer, 1vsOthers, ALLvsALL dungeon crawlers. The player needs to hold of the dark and mysterious void from swallowing the entire universe. To be able to werestand its power races from across the whole unverse has come to combine their powers and try to stop the void. Unsane consists of the following elements:

  • 15 playable characters, each having there own three unique abbilities. From which are 7 human and 8 inhuman.
  • 16 extrodinary creatures.
  • 4 diffrent play modes.
  • 3 diffrent diffuclty level.
  • 10 missions, excluding storymodes
  • 24 weapons that can be found. From which are 11 melee and 13 ranged.
  • Personal issues that can be solved and fears that can be triggerd.
  • A huge changeable board.
  • And much more, that can not be fit on this website...
